How to Use AI to Generate Appraisals and Evaluations

Appraisals and evaluations are some of the most high-stakes documents people write -- performance reviews, property assessments, business valuations, product critiques, vendor assessments -- and they are often produced under time pressure with no structured framework. AI has become a reliable first-draft partner for structured evaluation: give it the criteria, the evidence, and the context, and it produces a balanced, organized assessment that would take much longer to write from scratch. People use it to write performance reviews that are fair and specific, evaluate candidates or proposals against defined criteria, assess options before a decision, and produce structured feedback that is honest without being blunt. The output always benefits from human review, but the scaffold AI provides dramatically reduces the time and cognitive effort required.

5 Best Prompts for Generating Appraisals to Ask Claude or ChatGPT

Copy any prompt below and paste it directly into your AI of choice.

  1. Prompt 01 · Performance review

    "I need to write a performance review for [person] in the role of [role]. Here are the key things they did well this period: [list]. Here are the areas where they need to improve: [list]. Here are the goals they did or did not meet: [describe]. Can you draft a balanced, specific, professional review that is honest about both strengths and development areas?"

    Best for: performance reviews that are fair and evidence-based rather than vague or overly diplomatic.

  2. Prompt 02 · Evaluate options against criteria

    "I need to evaluate these options: [list options] against these criteria: [list criteria with relative importance]. Here is what I know about each option: [describe]. Can you produce a structured evaluation that scores or assesses each option against each criterion and gives me a clear recommendation with reasoning?"

    Best for: any decision involving multiple options where you want a structured, transparent comparison.

  3. Prompt 03 · Vendor or supplier assessment

    "I need to assess [vendor/supplier] for [purpose]. Here is the information I have about them: [describe]. The criteria that matter most to us are: [list]. Can you produce a structured assessment that identifies their strengths, weaknesses, and risks, and gives me a clear recommendation on whether to proceed?"

    Best for: procurement decisions where the stakes are high enough to warrant a formal assessment.

  4. Prompt 04 · Product or proposal critique

    "Here is [product / proposal / piece of work] I need to evaluate: [paste or describe]. Can you give me a structured critique covering: what works well and why, what does not work and why, what is missing, and an overall assessment of quality and fitness for purpose?"

    Best for: evaluating creative or strategic work with the rigor of a structured review rather than gut feeling.

  5. Prompt 05 · Self-appraisal

    "I need to write a self-appraisal for my [annual review / promotion application / performance discussion]. Here are the things I accomplished this year: [list]. Here are the challenges I faced: [describe]. Here are the skills I developed: [list]. Can you help me write a self-appraisal that is confident and specific without sounding boastful?"

    Best for: the self-appraisal that most people either undersell or write too vaguely.
