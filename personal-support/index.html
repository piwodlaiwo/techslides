Personal & Professional Support

Using AI for
Personal Support
& Wellbeing

Of all the ways people are using generative AI, the most striking shift in 2025 is deeply personal. People aren't just asking AI to write emails or fix code — they're turning to it for emotional support, life guidance, health questions, and the kind of conversations that used to require a therapist, a life coach, or a trusted friend. This section covers the human side of AI: the moments where the technology meets people where they are, helps them feel less alone, and quietly makes daily life a little more manageable.
