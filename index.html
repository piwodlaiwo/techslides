How People Are
Using Gen AI

Most people think of AI as a tool for writing emails faster or generating code. The reality is far more interesting. AI has moved well beyond productivity tools and coding assistants into deeply personal territory — therapy, purpose, creativity, and connection. People are using it to process grief, find direction, raise kids, negotiate salaries, learn languages, and ask the questions they've never felt safe asking anyone.
